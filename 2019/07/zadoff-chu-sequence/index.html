Zadoff-Chu Sequence

19.07.2019 - Zhennian Sun - Reading time ~1 Minute

Properties of Zadoff-chu sequence

  • Constant amplitude
  • Zero autocorrelation
  • Low cross-correlation

Low-PAPR sequence generation is 5G NR

The low-PAPR sequence $r{\mu,v}^{(\alpha,\delta)}(n)$ is defined by a cyclic shift $\alpha$ of a base sequence $\overline{r}{\mu,v}(n)$ according to

$$r{\mu,v}^{(\alpha,\delta)}(n)=e^{j\alpha n}\overline{r}{\mu,v}(n),0\leq n<M_{ZC}$$

$M_{ZC}$ is the length of sequence. Multiple sequences are defined from a single base sequence through different values of $\alpha$ and $\delta$.
